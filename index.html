How to Use StudyHub

🎯 Problem Generator

1. Select a subject from the dropdown menu

2. Click "Generate Problem" to receive a random question

3. Choose from 4 multiple choice options (1 correct, 3 incorrect)

4. Click "Reveal Step-by-Step Solution" to see detailed explanation

📝 Sketchbook

• Use the pen tool to draw derivations and chemical structures

• Switch tools with keyboard shortcuts: P for Pen, E for Eraser

• Add text notes with the "Add Text" button

• Download your sketches anytime with the download button

💾 Data Storage

All your sketches and problem history are saved locally in your browser.

No internet connection required after first load!

⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts

P - Switch to Pen | E - Switch to Eraser

C - Clear Canvas | Z - Undo (last action)

S - Save Sketch | D - Download
